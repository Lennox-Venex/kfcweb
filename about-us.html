Welcome to KFC

We're a team of passionate individuals dedicated to providing finger-lickin' good food to our customers. KFC was founded in 1952 by Colonel Harland Sanders and has since grown into one of the largest fast-food chains in the world.

Our History

KFC was founded in 1952 by Colonel Harland Sanders, who developed the secret recipe for our Original Recipe chicken. The first KFC restaurant opened in Corbin, Kentucky, and the company quickly expanded to become a national brand. Today, KFC operates over 24,000 locations in more than 140 countries.
